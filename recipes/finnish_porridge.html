Finnish Porridge for two

Description

Healthy Finnish-style porridge with cinnamon and banana.

Ingredients

  • Oats 80-120g
  • Salt (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Banana 1
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Boil water and add little bit salt to the water. Amount of water should be approximately 2.5dl per 40g of oats.
  2. Add oats to the water. Stir from time to time to avoid burning the porridge. Reduce heat so boiling is minimal.
  3. After a while (2-10 min, depending on Oats type) take the pot off oven.
  4. Serve porridge to bowls. Add chopped banana, cinnamon and sugar on top according to own taste.
