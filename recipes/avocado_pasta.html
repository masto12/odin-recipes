Avocado Pasta

Description

Recipe for 4 people who eat moderately large portions.

Ingredients

  • Pasta 600-700g (dry weight)
  • 3-4 Big avocados
  • Peccorino cheese 60-80g
  • Parmeggiano cheese 60-80g
  • 1-2 chilis
  • 2 limes
  • 2-4 garlic claws
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Put water boiling for pasta in water heater or pot.
  2. Chop chilis and garlic small, put them to a mixing bowl.
  3. Pour olive oil, black pepper and salt to bowl. Use salt according to how much cheese you intend to use as cheese enhances saltiness.
  4. Pour lime juice to the bowl with other ingridients.
  5. Chop and add avocados to bowl, mix well with other ingridients. Avocados can be either paste like or more solid depending on preference.
  6. Add salt to the boiling water in the pot. Take 1-2 dl of water from pot and add it to the mixing bowl.
  7. Add pasta to to the pot. Boil al dente.
  8. Add pecornio and parmegiano to the mixing bowl, mix well with other ingridients with wooden spoon. Leave some cheese to be added on top of the pasta portions.
  9. Cut and add few basil leaves to the mixing bowl.
  10. Mix bit by bit pasta to the mixing bowl and mix pasta with other ingridients. Be careful not to add too much pasta to avoid having poor pasta-to-sauce ratio.
  11. Serve immediately with cheese, the sauce gets quite black fast in freezer so it's not recommended.
