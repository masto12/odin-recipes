Mexican Mix

Recipe for mexican mix vegetarian meal for one person.

Ingredients

  • 50 - 100g Long rice
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Big tomato
  • 1 Can of black beans
  • 1/2 Lime
  • Shredded cheese (emmental, parmeggiano etc.)
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

  1. Put water boiling for rice.
  2. Chop avocado and tomato to dices. Add them to a serving plate.
  3. Add some black beans on plate. Aim is to have about equal portions of avocado-tomato-beans-rice but this can be modified according to own taste.
  4. Boil rice and add it to the plate once done.
  5. Pour lime juice on top of everything. Add some salt and pepper.
  6. Add cheese according to own taste and serve.
